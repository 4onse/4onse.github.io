Solar shield instructions
=========================

This part of the weather station manual is dedicated to the production of the
solar shield which is required to correctly measure temperature and humidity
parameters. Follow these steps:

#. Prepare six plant saucers (better if they are already white otherwise
   you should paint them) and a small junction box. Three threaded rod with
   3 mm diameter, bolts and spacers accordingly.

   +------------------+
   | |solar_shield_1| |
   +------------------+

#. Take one plant saucers and make a central hole to easily accomodate the junction box's
   size.

#. Take one plant saucer and make a centered hole of the junction box's
   size. Then, take other three plant saucers and do a centered hole of about 2 cm.

#. Take other three plant saucers and do a hole in the center of about 2 cm.

#. Make other three lateral small holes in all the disks which will be used to fit
   the threaded rod with the 3 mm diameter.
   (mark the corresponding holes on the different plates to let them correcty fit)

#. Follow the images to mount the solar shield:
